About The Library

Mission

The Library contributes to the mission of the college by engaging the community in collaborative stewardship - intentionally planning for the acquisition, care, management and accessibility of resources entrusted to our care. We strive to embody Olin's philosophy of continual improvement by exploring emergent technologies, practices, and information resources to serve Olin and the broader learning community.

Visit the Library

Our library is open to the public during staffed hours, typically 9am-5pm Monday-Friday. Please contact us in advance if you are looking for access to a particular resource so we can confirm availability. The library is located in Milas Hall. You may park in visitor parking, parking lot A.

Outside of staffed hours, only Olin students and keycard holders may be in the library. When hosting and events outside of normal hours, a Faculty or Staff member must be present at all times.
